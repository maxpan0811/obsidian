Astrill Setup Manual:How to configure PPTP with built-in client on Mac OS X
===========================================================================

Jump to: [navigation](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/Astrill_Setup_Manual:How_to_configure_PPTP_with_built-in_client_on_Mac_OS_X#mw-head),
[search](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/Astrill_Setup_Manual:How_to_configure_PPTP_with_built-in_client_on_Mac_OS_X#p-search)

**STEP 1**

---

Click on **System Preferences** icon in dock.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/92B2BA33-DE54-4C7A-8EB7-E49D92853110.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-001.jpg)

---

**STEP 2**

---

On **System Preferences** window, under **Internet & Network**, click on **Network** icon.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/E138D85E-401D-4252-91FE-907B33B87480.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-002.jpg)

---

**STEP 3**

---

On **Network** window, click the **plus (+)** button to create PPTP VPN connection.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/8C13B74C-9401-40DF-A1FB-340B762671E3.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-003.jpg)

---

**STEP 4**

---

For **Interface**, select **VPN**, for **VPN Type**, select **PPTP**, and for **Service Name**, type in **Astrill PPTP VPN**. When done, click on **Create** button.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/9F8AC16B-5C5C-494C-A964-4460F62423F1.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-004.jpg)

---

**STEP 5**

---

On **Network** screen, for **Server Name**, enter **GTX1.ASTRILL.NET**, and for **Account Name**, enter your **Astrill VPN account email**. When done, click on **Authentication Settings...** button. On **Members** section under **VPN Services** there is **[Servers List](https://members.astrill.com/servers-list.php)**. You can see all available servers which you can use.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/FCC1815B-4B29-456E-A371-BD95875449CC.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-005.jpg)

---

**STEP 6**

---

For **User Authentication**, select **Password**, and enter your **Astrill VPN account password**. When done, click on **OK** button, then click on **Advanced...** button.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/7C937B9F-008C-4C54-A32D-3C403AEA6F06.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-006.jpg)

---

**STEP 7**

---

Tick the box **Send all traffic over VPN connection**, then click on **OK** button.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/F2847FAD-40B9-4B42-BC4B-8ABE1560A967.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-007.jpg)

---

**STEP 8**

---

Click on **Apply** button, then to connect to Astrill PPTP VPN, click on **Connect** button.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/CD9D8E6C-85A8-44CD-B79E-7881A910A9D3.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-008.jpg)

---

**STEP 9**

---

Once you are connected, you will see **Status: Connected**, and to disconnect from Astrill PPTP VPN, simply click on **Disconnect** button.

[![](.evernote-content/BD3C5F2A-45AE-446D-8169-0409FD2E3590/76371F17-2AE7-4AA9-B722-4F63E560C4BD.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Mac-pptp-leopard-009.jpg)

---

***NOTE: When using PPTP/L2TP/IPSec VPN over 3G connection, make sure proxy settings are disabled. As it's likely these protocols can't work over proxy.***
