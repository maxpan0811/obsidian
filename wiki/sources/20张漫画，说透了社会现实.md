![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/679A4DCC-74FD-4BC4-A7DB-801E0050C451.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/1F942A2B-8374-4DA3-97DA-4353EF1FD228.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/0527306D-8661-4214-8675-CCEC529DD25A.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/2EA2D723-0898-4AA7-90A5-491F70B26CBF.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/DD9DC622-9767-4067-A1B1-70E30A18569E.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/565E1E3A-5F70-47F4-A836-590AA97CCE2E/E2088EA7-7326-4DCB-9C3F-24C10AE3F448.jpg)

▲代表公众利益的声音被禁锢，取而代之的便是个人利益。
