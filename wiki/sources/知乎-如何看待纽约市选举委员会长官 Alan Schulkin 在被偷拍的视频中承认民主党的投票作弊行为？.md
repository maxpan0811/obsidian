> 各党派会用车载着选民重复投票吗？可能吧。Schulkin先生并不能为他说的话提供任何证据。注意，他没有说“我看见了”或者“我们记录了（这一现象）”。所以，他也可以说：“相信我，火星上有小绿人，月亮是用奶酪做的！”我的意思是，大家想一想：官员是这个星球上仅次于明星，最受公众关注和细致检验的人。如果真的有人被巴士拉着投了很多次票，总有人会发现并把这事发到Youtube或者推特上。如果有人能找到这个人（Schulkin先生）并用秘密摄像头采访他，那跟踪一辆巴士就更没有问题了，更别说好几辆巴士了。  
> So do parties bus people around to vote multiple times? Maybe. Mr. Schulkin offers no evidence other than his position. Notice he doesn’t say “I’ve seen it happen,” or “we’ve documented it.” So he might as well be saying “trust me, there are little green men on mars and the moon is made of cheese.” I mean, think about it: public officials are amongst the most scrutinized and observed people on the planet, right after A list celebrities. If there really were a bunch of people traveling around on busses voting multiple times, somebody would figure it out and there would be stuff all over YouTube and Twitter. If somebody can track down this guy and interview him with a secret camera, they should have no trouble at all tracking down a bus — let alone several of them.  
> 归根结底，即使（选票作弊）确实存在，我也怀疑作弊的选票是否够改变总统选举的结果。可能这种作弊都不能改变州选举。这种作弊只可能会对地区选举有些影响。  
> The bottom line is that even if it is happening, I doubt it is happening enough to sway the election for president — and probably not even enough to sway state elections. But it could have an impact on local elections.  
> 2. 我觉得（Trump）是个脑子装满shi的种族歧视者。近年的选票作弊都是共和党的。（民主党）几乎没有选票作弊，且肯定不是视频中Schulkin说的那样。  
> I think he’s a racist shithead. The only voter fraud in recent years has been on the GOP side.
