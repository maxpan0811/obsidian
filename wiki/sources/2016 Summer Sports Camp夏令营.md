# 2016 Summer Sports Camp夏令营

---

2016 Summer Sports Camp夏令营
--------------------------

*2016-04-15*

We have a different “KIND”of camp!

We do offer sports. we do offer activities.

But we also offer character building to inspire kindness in your kids!

Weekly themes include:

caring / fairness / responsibility / honesty / and more!

Our professional, qualified instructors will provided classes and run activities for：

***swimming / football / tennis / karate / basketball / kindness teaching and many more!***

![](.evernote-content/112435A5-6908-4C5A-8321-0419B8DEAE29/5B5172C8-0279-48E9-9A89-690E93DCB03C.jpg)

SportsWorld童励 camps focus on encouraging an active lifestyle through sport and overall character development, along with many more fun activities for kids.

**【Date】2016/6.20-8.19**

**【Time】9:00-15:00**

 ( 9 weeks of Monday - Friday sessions to choose from )

**Ages:4-12**

Equipment to prepare: 

Swimming equipment, tennis rackets, hat, water bottle,  and long sleeves / pants ( Adventure Zone children’s slide)
