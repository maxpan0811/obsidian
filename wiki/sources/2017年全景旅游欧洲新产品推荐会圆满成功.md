2017年全景旅游欧洲新产品推荐会圆满成功
---------------------

*2017-11-19*[旅行社资讯网](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MjM5MDM0MzY0MA==&mid=2650045228&idx=2&sn=b0b3704d98136f91a8f10aadbbedb87c&chksm=be46939b89311a8df3b2e2a3abafceb2239e12d8a5adb7821e679cba3daaac444fffa21dd818&mpshare=1&scene=1&srcid=11199BaYNCc5A79RLOI8xCNB##)
