Google lets you add notes, lists, and images from Google Keep directly into your Google Docs and Slides. Use existing notes or create a new one and add it on the fly without ever leaving your file. Here’s how.

For this guide, we’ll be using [Google Docs](https://www.howtogeek.com/420971/the-beginners-guide-to-google-docs/), but this method works identically on [Slides](https://www.howtogeek.com/428140/the-beginners-guide-to-google-slides/) as well.

**RELATED:** [***The Beginner's Guide to Google Docs***](https://www.howtogeek.com/420971/the-beginners-guide-to-google-docs/)

Fire up your browser and [head to Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/). Open a new or existing document and then click the [Google Keep](https://www.howtogeek.com/198829/how-to-use-google-keep-for-frustration-free-note-taking/) icon located in the pane to the right side of the page.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/675B955D-0C13-481A-9E19-9E21C02A2738.png)

From the pane that opens, hover over the note you want to add to your document. Click the three-dot button and then select “Add to Document.”

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/DAC65CC0-4FA8-4BDA-BA6A-E4D36F2C3627.png)

The content of the Keep note gets inserted into your document wherever the text cursor was located.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/AF1148DC-E926-45FB-ABC6-9285F34CAEBC.png)

If you don’t have any notes in Keep, click either “Take a Note” or the list icon to create a note or list, respectively.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/F1CA18A9-549A-488A-B499-0C90DBBE84C4.png)

After you finish, click “Done.”

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/F9E4544C-6C52-48F5-BA29-1582392BBC0A.png)

Click the three-dot button and then select “Add to Document” just like before to add the note to your file.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/CEC6EA9F-883A-428C-9BB1-77226AEE53F3.png)

You can also drag and drop any note in the right panel directly into your document to add it to a specific spot in your file.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/2B495F0B-82C8-480F-A677-8958220A24F8.png)

If your note contains images, they will also be added to your file.

![](.evernote-content/43620E5E-1F6E-43D9-B338-6D0A4B240853/A35E2A87-0D1C-4E14-B060-D22E13277C05.png)

---

That’s all there is to it. Adding your Google Keep notes to your Docs and Sheet files has never been easier.
