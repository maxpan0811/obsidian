万科双语学校名誉校长王石：秉承传统，拥抱世界
----------------------

*2015-10-21*

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/21F321A6-BF06-4762-8CBB-62571C8250A7.jpg)

很多第一次来万科上海中心的人，都会感叹它不像办公楼。走过一大片池塘和草坪，大型玻璃立面里面，是动态的协作区域，万科员工新奇和创新的想法，在这里不断涌现。

2016年9月，这里将变成一个更加生动活泼的空间，上海万科双语学校的学生，将在这栋建筑内，通过探索和发明去学习。

过去30年，作为房地产企业的万科，用建筑赞美生命；未来的日子里，进入教育领域的万科，将竭力为孩子打造美好的心灵家园。

2011年，我开始了海外游学之旅。哈佛游学两年半后，又去了剑桥游学。通过切身感受和比较，发现传统的中式教育强调权威，压抑了孩子的个性；而西方人从孩子五六岁开始，就培养他们的学习热情、自信心和创造精神，培养一生持之以恒的知识学习和身体锻炼。

我感觉，一所好学校，会有一种特殊的磁场，让人祛除浮躁，静心读书。中国孩子是很聪明的，只要有了环境和意识，会获取全新的成长。

万科双语学校的学术班底，来自拥有19年历史的上海知名民办中小学——复旦万科实验学校，该校实践双语教育长达10年。新学校建成后，将成为世界上为数不多的几所将中外籍学生混合编班的双语学校。沉浸式的语言学习，多元文化的融合，让学生不出国门，也能拥抱世界。

赛艇精神将是这所学校校园文化的重要组成部分。这项运动要求所有队员高度配合、互相信任，身体和心灵高度合一，保持持久的耐力和聚焦。世界名校的赛艇队员往往是成绩优异的学生，每天运动，再去学习，你的注意力会非常集中，学习效率会非常高。这是体育给人的一种特别内力。

除现有的社区配套学校外，万科双语学校会是上海万科第一所自办的中小学。我非常荣幸可以担任学校名誉校长，也期待看到万科上海中心的改变。以前，这里是建筑大师、新锐设计师出没的地方。未来，希望这里将产生科学家、文学家、科学家、体育选手……更重要的是产生改变世界的未来领袖。

让我们一起攀登这座知识的山峰吧！

上海万科双语学校并不是一所新学校

Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School is not a new school.

它的前身是复旦万科实验学校“国际双语C班”，在读学生来自美国、加拿大、英国、新加坡、韩国、港澳台、中国大陆等近20个国家和地区。

Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School grew out of the International Bilingual C Program of Fudan Vanke Experimental Private School. At present, there are students from 20 countries and regions, including the United States, Canada, Britain, Singapore, South Korea, Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, and Mainland China.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/039B62D2-AED6-4B36-9ACE-2B95A2B88C06.jpg)

国际双语C班创立于2006年，旨在融合中西方教育精髓，培养根植中国、具有国际视野和国际交往能力的优秀学生。独具特色的国际课程，结合了东方严谨的治学风格与西方轻松的课堂氛围，采用东方扎实的基础教育与西方科学的评价方式。中英文教学时间各占50%，外教担任班主任，中教担任助教。

Founded in 2006, International Bilingual C Program aims to merge the Chinese education essence with Western education essence. We cultivate students the international perspective and international communication ability in hand while rooting in Chinese culture. The Chinese teaching and English teaching respectively accounts for 50%. The foreign teachers take charge of homeroom teachers and the Chinese teachers teaching assistants.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/B5A58F21-FDC2-4D7E-AC06-05143C8329D1.jpg)

20人的小班化教学，使得师生关系亲密、和谐，每一个孩子都能得到老师的关心和鼓励。

Small class size with 20 students contributes to an intimate and harmonious teacher-student relationship. Every child is respected as an individual learner. They are fully cared and encouraged by the adult staff at school.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/2BD8E3A9-56FD-4FB5-A977-3DC79C2EFBDC.jpg)

经过九年实践探索，国际双语C班培养出的学生既具有良好的学习习惯，又具备多元化思维能力。 6年级学生的中英文水平几乎达到“双母语”，学生中文能力考核，在闵行区成绩名列前茅。英语能力考核以美国ITBS考试为主， 根据历史测试成绩表明，国际双语C班每个年级学生的英语水平，都高于美国同年龄学生的平均水平。 9年级学生的测试成绩，已超过美国10年级的平均水平。

Through nine years of practice and exploration, students are equipped with not onlygood learning habits, but also diversified thinking ability. The Chinese-English language ability of the grade-6 students has reached the level of twin mother-tongues. In Chinese language ability examination, the students are among the best of Minhang district. The English proficiency assessment of the students is based on the American ITBS. According to the historical test performance, students of every grade are better than American students of the same age in English proficiency. The test scores of Grade 9 students exceed the average scores of the American Grade 10 students.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/0AE8A7CF-B48C-43DD-96D7-F470A8053594.png)

上海万科双语学校又是一个崭新的学校

Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School is a new school.

往年，国际双语C班仅招收20名一年级新生，其他年级招收少量插班生。 但是每年报名学生远远超过招生人数，有许多双语均佳的学生因为人数限制被拒之门外。

Inprevious years, International Bilingual C Program only recruited 20 students for grade one and a few transfer students for other grades. The application students are far above the enrollment, among who are good at both languages.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/A6DD6026-0B92-446D-AB7D-E032002D3EE3.jpg)

为让更多学生有机会享受高品质的双语教育，“国际双语C班”模式将被完全引入万科双语学校，原有全部师资班底和在读学生，均在2016年9月搬入新建设的上海万科双语学校，同时招收一年级、六年级新生以及各年级插班生，学生总人数将达到300人以上。

Inorder to provide more opportunities for students applying for this acceleratedbilingual school, Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School will fully introduce theInternational Bilingual C Program into its new campus. All of the original team of teachers and students will move into the newly-built Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School by September, 2016. The school will enroll students of Grade 1 and 6, and some students of other grades. The total student number will reach above 300.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/5971AA5F-5C49-4C96-9979-2C8928FBF042.jpg)

上海万科双语学校创校校长由现任复旦万科实验学校校长、“国际双语C班”创立人陈舒担任，学术总监由“国际双语C班”外籍创立人Ms. Lisa Chisholm担任。

ChenShu, the current Principal of Shanghai Fudan-Vanke Experimental Private Schooland the founder of International Bilingual C Program, serves as the founder and Head of Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School. Ms. Lisa Chisholm, the foreign founderof International Bilingual C Program, takes charge of Academic Director in Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/DD207CFC-04AD-4535-BDE8-A4BF653C0005.jpg)

校长、“国际双语C班”创立人陈舒

学术总监、“国际双语C班”外籍创立人Ms. Lisa Chisholm

上海万科双语学校的办学宗旨，在原复旦万科实验学校“成长无限快乐”的基础上，延伸出“激发无限可能”的办学使命。学校将通过丰富而赋予挑战的双语多文化综合课程，培养孩子全面平衡的个性、终生学习的能力、创新领导的精神，为创造孩子健康丰盛的人生奠定基础。

The mission of Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School is to Inspire Unlimited Potential(IUP). Through the rich and challenging bilingual, multicultural and integrated curriculum, we are devoted to cultivating students’well-balanced personality, lifelong learning ability and innovative leadership, thus laying a solid foundation for a sound and fruitful life for all our students.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/FBFCA5DC-2AEC-49D9-8E51-C4947927DED2.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/BC73CAE8-A356-4B4E-9E86-AEFFAC48AFEA.jpg)![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/F48B1BD2-570F-4B10-8690-3C33A67B72BD.jpg)

上海万科双语学校的落成，将成为上海万科从房地产品牌走向教育品牌的第一步。校园选址在原万科上海中心，由万科顶尖设计师团队改建成校园，其照明、空气循环、隔音系统等，均参考美国、日本及中国台湾地区的标准，达到全球最高学校建筑设计水准。

Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School upholds the educational philosophy of integrating Chinese education essence to Western education essence. The school adheres the educational aims of inspiring unlimited potential. Based on the former curricula of International Bilingual C Program, the curriculum of Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School will be comprehensively renovated and improved to shape unique curriculum system which not only retains the original high standard of English-Chinese bilingual courses, but also develops new courses for fostering students'critical thinking and creativity. These courses will include STEAM courses, sports, Arts and courses in other aspects. Moreover extracurricular activities, like rowing, Run for Love, Industry Summer Career Practice Projects with Vanke characteristics, will expand a lot so as to construct a unique bilingual, multicultural and integrated curriculum.

![](.evernote-content/9DD77A79-E6F2-4972-8BCC-99850521FC46/CE33B58E-8626-48D9-8271-3B88EA03FD1A.jpg)

上海万科双语学校的课程将秉承东西教育精髓融合的教育理念，激发无限潜能的教育宗旨，对原国际双语C班课程进行全面修整和提升，形成独创的课程体系，既保留原高标准的中英双语课程，又新开设旨在培养学生批判性思维和创造能力的智慧课程、STEAM课程、体艺类课程等，同时课外活动板块将得到更多的拓展，加入万科特色的赛艇、乐跑、行业暑假实践项目等，形成独特的双语多文化综合课程。

Shanghai Vanke Bilingual School upholds the educational philosophy of integrating Chinese education essence to Western education essence.
