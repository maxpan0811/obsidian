2017 AJA Creative Summer Camp
-----------------------------

**AJA Summer Camp**

**今夏玩转创意夏令营就在**

**宜乐儿童之家**

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/C40137D7-B896-4298-BC6D-02E99CDB4B12.png)

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/66F25C18-2DB5-434D-A680-73877E6B87B3.jpg)

For the past four years, Angel Joy Academy has offered academically challenging and fun-filled educational Summer Camp. This year, we proudly to once again offer English and Chinese Summer Camp Program. Our main focus is to serve and provide children with the highest educational standard possible in a fun, warm and loving environment.

在过去的四年里，宜乐儿童之家一直不间断地为孩子们提供具有学业挑战性和教学趣味性的双料夏令营。今年，我们再次隆重推出AJA创意双语夏令营。这个暑假在AJA的夏令营里孩子们不仅能收获到满满的知识，更重要的是感受到我校充满爱意与创意的浓浓教学氛围。

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/2BC5AC05-53F1-4E15-846A-AB78DEFC8C78.jpg)

AJA Creative Camp allows children to discover and learn both in and outside the classroom. The program includes Reading, Creative English Workshop, Science, Chinese Language/Culture, Arts and Crafts, Cooking, Music, Water Fun and Physical Education.

        AJA创意夏令营允许孩子们在课堂内外展开探索和学习。内容包括创意英文工作室，双语阅读，科学实验，中国语言及文化，艺术手工，烘培烹饪，音乐欣赏，水上娱乐以及体育项目活动。

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/E1FDB551-28B6-45B6-BBEB-8E7A45FDA398.jpg)

Our unique fun filled English immersion class will include reading clubs with age appropriate reading selection that support reading readiness.

        我校特有的趣味英文浸入式课程涵盖了适合各个年龄阶段的阅读俱乐部，为孩子将来的独立阅读能力打下了良好的基础。

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/78F86636-3F21-4C81-8CCA-E61A45776AE7.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/EA2EC265-E950-4A40-AEF0-0412CAA5A8FD.jpg)

AJA’s unique curriculum will integrate subjects such as literacy, Chinese and science with art. In addition, the children will be involved in fun science experiments such as balloon rocket, making crystal, color changing flowers, rainbow jar, simple electricity, simple machine: Archimedes’ screw and lifting jack.

        AJA独特的教学大纲整合了英语读写能力，中文，科学及艺术等学科。孩子们有机会参与到各种有趣的科学实验中来：如气球火箭，制作晶体，变色花，彩虹罐。并接触到简单的电力和基础机械知识：如阿基米德螺旋和千斤顶。

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/F5D04980-E7B2-416F-9ABF-E0F029EFA6F9.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/6ACC130B-C2D1-4AA1-AAD8-ADF29F500C0C.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/1AFEBF90-88A2-46F3-BAA8-DFEE809FD93A.jpg)

AJA’s Chinese Summer Curriculum will include calligraphy, Chinese painting, paper cutting and music. And to keep your children healthy and active, our sport summer camp program includes yoga, kung-fu, Zumba, water fun, soccer and basketball.

        AJA创意夏令营的中文课程将包括中国书法，国画，剪纸以及传统音乐欣赏。同时为了保持孩子们的健康和活力，我校的体育课程将设置瑜伽，功夫，尊巴舞，水上娱乐，足球和篮球。

![](.evernote-content/DA1B3AC4-D8E6-4309-9C93-181E708D7CFD/26BCAA2B-9E3D-40E1-902C-1F78A2B4BCB8.jpg)

宜乐儿童之家是一所结合蒙特梭利教学方法的双语国际幼儿学校，为2-6岁的儿童提供学龄前的教育。AJA拥有安全的室内和室外游乐空间，每间教室的设计都是从孩子的兴趣和安全出发。AJA的教师团队由资深的外教团队和中教团队组成，尊重孩子的个性特点，课程与活动一切都从孩子的需要出发。除了中、英文双语课程，我们还提供丰富多彩的兴趣班，如：乐高、阅读、功夫，创意戏剧、音乐启蒙、芭蕾舞蹈、艺术手工、轮滑、生日派对等课程和活动。AJA相信双语立体国际化的思维模式加上形式多样的兴趣班课程，能给孩子提供更大的发展空间。
