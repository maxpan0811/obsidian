奥巴马于 2009 年就任美国第 43 任美国总 任美国总 统之初，便并颁布《美国振兴及投资法案》以及“竞争卓越”计划（ Race to the Top ），强调科学 、科技、工程及数， （science，technology，engineering  and mathematic ，简称 STEM）的重要性，并拨款大力资助 STEM教育。

The U.S. President Obama has consistently called for improvements in STEM education to move America’s students to the top of the pack by enabling all students to learn deeply and think critically in science and math; expanding STEM education opportunities for students from all backgrounds; and building partnerships among educators, businesses and community partners to support advances in STEM education. This Administration has promoted several successful STEM initiatives, including prioritizing STEM education in Race to the Top and the Investing in Innovation Fund; improving the coordination of STEM education initiatives between the Department of Education and NSF; and promoting over 100 industry partners in their efforts to boost STEM learning through Change the Equation.

万科教育从美国独家引进机器人学院的课程，将 STEM 创新教育结合，用科技和娱乐的教学方式颠覆传统育法，建立青少年教育的未来新标准，我们希望孩子能：

We define the standards of the future education. At the Robotix Institute we combine Robotics and Games to provide top notch STEM education to children around the world. Students will learn STEM and creativity through the revolutionary technology and interactive experience. We guide students to

**课程安排**

**Sample Schedule**

小天才初级营 Beginner Level
---------------------

(适合：6-8岁   Ages 6-8)
--------------------

D1：背景知识介绍，设计机器人，完成组装机器人   

Introduce robotics background, design robots, build basic robots  

D2:零部件介绍及在机器人上的简易编程  

Introduce components. Make easy programs on the robot brick.

D3:马达编程，图像编程，声音编程，音乐编程       

Programming Large Motor, Medium Motor, Image Programming, Sound Programmi ng, Make Anim  ation and Music

D4:触碰传感器编程，颜色/光传感器、超声波传感器编程，方向传感器编程

Touch Sensor, Color/Light Sensor, Ultrasonic Sensor, Gyro Sensor

D5:终极挑战任务，颁奖及闭营仪式  

Final Challenge, Show Time, Awards Time, Camp Graduation Ceremony

小天才中级营 Medium Level
-------------------

(适合：6-8岁   Ages 6-8)
--------------------

D1：设计组装创意部件一，介绍更多零件及STEM知识，复习马达和触碰传感器编程.

Design and Build. Introduce more pieces and STEM knowledge. Review Motor Programming and Touch Sensor

D2：介绍更多零件及STEM知识，复习超声波和方向传感器编程                           

Design and Build. Introduce more pieces and STEM knowledge. Review Ultrasonic Sensor and Gyro Sensor

D3：设计组装创意部件三，介绍更多零件及STEM知识，复习颜色传感器编程       

Design and Build. Introduce more pieces and STEM knowledge. Review Color/Light Sensor

D4：数学模块，文字处理模块，按钮控制，比较模块，范围模块，随机数模块     

Math Block, Text Blog, Button Block, Compare Block, Range Block, Random Block

D5：终极挑战任务，颁奖及闭营仪式

Final Challenge, Show Time, Awards Time, Camp Graduation Ceremony

小极客初级营 Beginner Level
---------------------

(适合：9-14岁   Ages 9-14)
----------------------

D1：背景知识介绍，设计机器人，完成组装机器人，零部件介绍 

Introduce robotics background, design robots, build robots, introduce components

D2：马达编程，触碰传感器编程，循环、条件、分支等编程逻辑

 Large Motor, Medium Motor, Touch Sensor, Loop, Switch-Case

D3：颜色/光传感器、超声波传感器编程，路线跟踪编程专题 

Color/Light Sensor, Ultrasonic Sensor, Line Following

D4：逻辑思维、工程概念和方法应用实践，终极挑战任务

Programming Logic, Engineering Concepts and Methods, prepare Final Challenge

D5：终极挑战任务分析，颁奖及闭营仪式

Final Challenge Show Time, Awards Time, Camp Graduation Ceremony

(适合：9-14岁   Ages 9-14)

Variable, Array and other advanced blocks

Build, Analyze, Find out solution, Draw Flow Chart, Start Programming

D3:完成基础编程，改造提升机器人

Program, Test and Optimize

D2:完成组装
