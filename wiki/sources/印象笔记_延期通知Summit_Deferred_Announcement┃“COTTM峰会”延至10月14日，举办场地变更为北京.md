**“COTTM峰会”延期通知**

鉴于近期新冠疫情反复，原定于2021年9月14日在北京东苑戏楼举办的“COTTM峰会”将延期至2021年10月14日，举办场地变更为北京长城饭店二层大宴会厅（北京市朝阳区东三环北路10号）。

对此，我们诚挚向境外目的地旅游局/部、旅游业者和合作伙伴深表歉意！

疫情当下、世事维艰，中国出境旅游交易会（COTTM）将依旧为境外旅游目的地与中国旅游业者提供行业资源和信息对接而坚守一份责任。

COTTM 团队

2012年8月26日

**COTTM Summit - Deferred to 14th October 2021**

The COTTM Summit will now take place on Thursday 14th October 2021 at the Great Wall Hotel, Beijing.

The COTTM Summit was originally due to take place on 14th September 2021.  With safety being our main priority and following consultation with all of our key stakeholders, we are confirming the COTTM Summit will now take place on 14th October 2021. 

We do apologise to the overseas destination Tourism Boards/Ministries of Tourism, Tour Operators and all of our supporting partners.

As the organiser of China Outbound Travel & Tourism Market (COTTM) we will continue to strive in our responsibility to provide the industry with resources and connections between overseas destinations and Chinese tour operators.

Thank you for your support.

COTTM Team.
