### Customer Reference Number (CRN): 09946070

COVID-19 UPDATE:

At *The Economist*, we’re prioritising the health and well-being of our staff which means the resource at our Contact Centres will be reduced. We’re updating our troubleshooting guides and FAQs on a regular basis, so please do check our [Help Pages](https://www.economist.com/help/home) before getting in touch.

For more information, please see our regularly updated FAQs for [covid-19](https://www.economist.com/help/covid19).

To manage your account online, including updating payment details and changing your address, please visit the [My Account](https://my.economist.com/user#details) page. If you are a new customer or this link does not work, you will first need to visit [www.economist.com/activate](https://www.economist.com/activate). You will need your Customer Reference Number (CRN) and email address to create an account.

To discuss a more affordable or flexible subscription package, the best way to contact us is via Chat, which can be found in the [Help and Support](https://my.economist.com/user#help) section in your My Account page. Chat is available 8:30am - 5:30pm GMT, 7 days a week and you will need to [sign in or register](https://www.economist.com/register) for a free online account first.

Our UK Contact Centre is closed due to the covid-19 restrictions in place, therefore we are working at a reduced level.

Our Asia Contact Centre is closed due to the covid-19 curfews in place, therefore we are working at a severely reduced level and reduced opening times.

If you do need to contact us, please ensure you include your Customer Reference Number (CRN) and the full name on the subscription. During this exceptional time, there may be a delay in our response to you, but we will endeavour to reply within 3 working days.

For enquiries about your subscription that can’t be managed through our [Help Pages](https://www.economist.com/help/home) and your [My Account](https://my.economist.com/user#details) page, please contact our Subscription team.

If you have a query about your digital access or a login issue, please read through our [troubleshooting steps](https://www.economist.com/help/techspecs) first to help resolve this. If you do contact our Digital team, they will talk you through the same steps.

If you’ve signed up via iTunes or Google Play and would like to cancel, please contact [Apple App Support](https://support.apple.com/apps) or [Google Play Help](https://support.google.com/googleplay/?hl=en) directly regarding this, as we can not refund or extend any subscription paid through a third party.
