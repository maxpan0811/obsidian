湖乱跑 YOUNG ATHLETE 为湖乱跑赛事品牌旗下青少年跑步品牌，致力于培养国内青少年跑步文化。自2015年开始，湖乱跑即在千岛湖马拉松中设立4.2公里家庭亲子马拉松，随后在2016年汇添富南京山地马拉松中又增加12公里的家庭亲子组别。

![](.evernote-content/6AC659E1-D7DD-4A38-99B2-D85013A56E75/158D572A-83D9-48B9-B5BD-9D57EFAB39F0.jpg)

经过多年对青少年赛事的探索并不断积累的经验，在未来，湖乱跑 YOUNG ATHLETE 将推出更多青少年相关的赛事，包括湖乱跑 YOUNG ATHLETE 独立赛事，以及嵌入上海穹景体育旗下各类赛事中，如Ninghai Classic / Ultra Trail Ninghai / Lijiang Adventure等。

![](.evernote-content/6AC659E1-D7DD-4A38-99B2-D85013A56E75/7BB9771E-E01F-41A8-B86E-8371E2067FA2.jpg)

 

湖乱跑 YOUNG ATHLETE 首场专属于青少年的越野赛事，2017 杭州青少年越野赛 WE ARE YOUNG 今日上午10 : 00开放报名啦！本次青少年越野赛赛道距离为9.6公里，赛事成绩将按照青少年的成绩排名，且分年龄组别。具体信息如下。

**一、主办单位**

上海穹景体育文化发展有限公司

**二、协办单位**

杭州千禧度假酒店 

**三、竞赛日期及地点**

2017年7月16日 ( 星期日 ) 上午7点在杭州千禧度假酒店举行

 

**四、竞赛项目**

1. WE ARE YOUNG 9.6公里亲子跑，累计爬升424米，下降416米

2. 参赛名额200组（ 400人 ）

\*分8岁 - 13岁和14岁 - 18岁（ 未满 ）两个年龄段

Huluanpao YOUNG ATHLETE is a youth running brand that subordinates to Huluanpao. It dedicates to foster the running culture among Chinese adolescents. Huluanpao started to set up 4.2km family run event in the Thousand Island Lake Marathon in 2015 and then started to set up 12km family run event in CUAM Nanjing Mountain Marathon in 2016.

 

After years of exploration and accumulating experiences in organizing junior races, Huluanpao YOUNG ATHLETE will organize more races for teenagers in the future, in the form of separate Huluanpao YOUNG ATHLETE junior races or in the form of a part of the other Skyview races, like Ninghai Classic, Ultra Trail Ninghai, Lijiang Adventure and so on.

Registration for 2017 Hangzhou Junior Trail Race WE ARE YOUNG, the first separate junior trail race of Huluanpao YOUNG ATHLETE, will open at 10 a.m. this morning. The race will cover a course of 9.6km. The result will be ranked by race time in each age  division.Please find the information below.

**I. Host**

Shanghai Skyview Sport Co., Ltd.

 

**II. Co - operation**

Millennium Resort Hangzhou

 

**III. Race Date and Location**

7 a.m. Sunday, July 16, 2017 at Millennium Resort Hangzhou

 

**IV. Event**

WE ARE YOUNG 9.6km Family Run, with an aggregate elevation gain of 424m and an aggregate elevation loss of 416m.

The race entries are limited to 200 teams ( 400 people ).

\* There are two age groups, the group of 8 - 13 and the one of 14- 18 under.
