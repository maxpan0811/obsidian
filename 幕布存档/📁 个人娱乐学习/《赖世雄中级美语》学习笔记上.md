# <span>习惯用法</span>
- <span>感官动词+宾语+宾语补足语（do/doing/done）</span>
  - <span>看：see/watch/look at</span>
  - <span>听：hear/listen to</span>
  - <span>感觉：feel</span>
  - <span>动词原形做补足语</span>
    - <span>I saw her leave.</span>
    - <span>I heard him cry.</span>
  - <span>现在分词做宾语补足语</span>
    - <span>I saw her leaving.</span>
    - <span>I heard him crying.</span>
    - <span>I felt my legs trembling.</span>
  - <span>过去分词做宾语补足语</span>
    - <span>I saw him robbed.</span>
    - <span>I heard the door shut.</span>
    - <span>I felt myself lifted.</span>
- <span>介词不能直接加that从句</span>
  - <span>介词+the fact+that从句</span>
    - <span>I am worried about that he doesn’t study.（❌）</span>
    - <span>I am worried about that fact that he dosen’t study.（✔️）</span>
  - <span>be动词+形容词+that从句</span>
    - <span>I am sure of that the team won the game.（❌）</span>
    - <span>I am sure that the team won the game.（✔️）</span>
  - <span>置于be动词之后做表语</span>
    - <span>The truth is that nobody really cares what you do,</span>
- <span>反问句</span>
  - <span>与祈使句使用时，反问句一律用 will you</span>
    - <span>Shut up, will you?</span>
  - <span>与“Let‘s”句型使用时，反问句一律用shall we</span>
    - <span>Let’s go dancing, shall we?</span>
