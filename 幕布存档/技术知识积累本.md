# <span>Mac</span>
- <span>open the macbook or connect to power without the laptop turning on</span>
  - <span>都不开机：sudo nvram BootPreference=%00</span>
  - <span>打开盖子不开机：sudo nvram BootPreference=%01</span>
  - <span>插上电源不开机：sudo nvram BootPreference=%02</span>
  - <span>恢复缺省状态：sudo nvram -d BootPreference</span>
- <span>wav文件转化为m4a文件</span>
  - <span>for f in *.wav; do afconvert "$f" -o "${f%.wav}.m4a" -f m4af -d aac; done</span>
