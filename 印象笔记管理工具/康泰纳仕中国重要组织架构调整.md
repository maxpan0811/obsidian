# 康泰纳仕中国重要组织架构调整

---

康泰纳仕中国重要组织架构调整
--------------

康泰纳仕

![](.evernote-content/8FBFAD64-A674-4D1A-BB22-7D0CCD5175A3/2955E80F-5F46-4D9B-A689-71BA78D862BF.jpg)

**各位重要的合作伙伴，**

 

康泰纳仕中国在此公布一个重要组织架构调整：唐杰（Paco Tang）将于2019年7月1日起担任集团运营总监，负责《服饰与美容VOGUE》，《智族GQ》和《悦游Condé Nast Traveler》的商业运营。

 

康泰纳仕正在进行全球整合，此外有部分重要市场例如美国和意大利，采用了跨品牌商业整合的管理模式。正如康泰纳仕国际公司总裁Wolfgang Blau表示：“媒体行业正在持续经历着数字时代所带来的冲击和变化，未来在中国市场取得更大成功的关键，是让我们旗下重要媒体品牌持续创新及整合，同时坚持各个品牌的独特个性，面向不同的受众群体，以达到公司业务快速发展的目的。”

 

康泰纳仕中国管理层决定指派唐杰（Paco Tang）担任集团运营总监，负责《服饰与美容VOGUE》，《智族GQ》和《悦游Condé Nast Traveler》。在此，特别感谢《服饰与美容VOGUE》编辑总监Angelica Cheung张宇女士在本次决策过程中的参与和支持。

 

Paco自2017年9月1日起担任《智族GQ》运营总监，在过去近两年的时间里，他和《智族GQ》编辑总监曾鸣、新媒体总监Rocco及团队成员共同努力，将品牌提升到一个全新的高度，成为最受欢迎和最具价值的媒体品牌之一。我们深信Paco是担任这个重要角色的最佳人选。今后Paco也将与Angelica紧密合作，让《服饰与美容VOGUE》这个中国最具影响力的时尚媒体品牌不断创新。

 

《服饰与美容VOGUE》运营总监李宝剑因个人原因向公司提出辞职，将于2019年6月30日正式离开。在过去这一年里，宝剑带领商业团队，与内容团队密切合作，打造出多元立体化的品牌声量，获得行业内的广泛认可。我们衷心感谢宝剑所做出的贡献。

 

作为行业引领者，康泰纳仕中国通过此次组织结构调整，促使更深入的业务整合，为公司的发展注入全新的活力。我们将继续以创新为动力、优质内容为核心，为消费者以及商业合作伙伴带来更大的价值。

 

如您有任何问题，请随时与我们联系。

 

最后，我们衷心感谢您一如既往的大力支持。我们期待与您携手共创更加美好的未来！

**Sophia Liao**

**康泰纳仕中国区总裁**

**2019年6月20日**

![](.evernote-content/8FBFAD64-A674-4D1A-BB22-7D0CCD5175A3/2955E80F-5F46-4D9B-A689-71BA78D862BF.jpg)

**Dear valued partners,**

 

Today Condé Nast China announces a significant organisational change. Beginning July 1, 2019, Paco Tang will become Group Operation Director responsible for driving business operations of VOGUE, GQ and Condé Nast Traveler in China.

 

Conde Nast is in the process of global integration. A few key markets including US and Italy have adopted an integrated commercial model across their respective brand portfolios. Wolfgang Blau, President of Condé Nast International commented: "The Media industry continues to experience disruption and changes in this highly dynamic digitalised era. Ensuring that our most important brands can innovate and collaborate whilst retaining their individual identities and specific audiences to accelerate their presence in China, is important to the company's future success."

 

The management of Conde Nast China has decided to appoint Paco Tang to become the Group Operation Director, across VOGUE, GQ and Condé Nast Traveler in China. I want to recognise and thank VOGUE China Editorial Director Angelica Cheung, for her support and involvement in this process.

 

Paco was named Operation Director of GQ China in September 2017. Over the past two years, through the joint efforts with Editorial Director Zeng Ming, Digital Director Rocco Liu and the whole GQ team, the Brand has been uplifted to a new height, becoming one of the most favoured and highly valuable media brands in China. We firmly believe Paco is the right talent for this important role. In his new role, Paco will work closely with Angelica to continuously innovate VOGUE, the most influential fashion media brand in China.

 

Li Baojian has resigned from his position as Operation Director VOGUE China due to personal reasons and will leave Condé Nast China on June 30, 2019. Baojian has been widely recognised for enhancing the collaboration between the editorial and commercial teams, promoting the VOGUE brand through multi-dimensional campaigns. We are sincerely grateful to Baojian for his contribution to VOGUE China over the past year.

 

As the market leader, Condé Nast China is taking this organisational change to create an integrated and revitalised environment that continues to innovate and offer the best quality content for our users and strategic business solutions for our partners.

 

Should you have any question, please do not hesitate to contact us. 

 

Finally, we would like to extend our heartfelt gratitude to you for your strong and continuous support. We look forward to creating a brighter future with you together! 

 

Yours sincerely,

***Sophia Liao***

**President, Condé Nast China**

**June 20, 2019**
