Group Fare Contract
===================

甲方:
英国航空公司                                                                             
以下简称 [英航]

British Airways                                                                     in following referred to as ‘BA’

乙方:
北京XXXXXXXXXX公司                                                                        以下简称
[旅行社]
 

(IATA#XXXXXXXX)                                                               in following referred to as ‘AGENCY’

日期: 2019年
X月
X 日

第**1**条
合同条款

Paragraph 1 Terms of Contract
=============================

1.  
就于
2020 年
4 月
1 日至
2020 年
9 月
30 日承诺
80%的团体成行事宜，英航与旅行社达成一致协议。

BA and AGENCY enter an agreement on group sales of 80% commitment from 01APR2020 to 30SEP2020.

2. 
旅行社将从英航收到团体的净票价和条款细则。

AGENCY will receive net fare and conditions from BA.

3. 
旅行社将以电邮确认合同并回覆到英航，旅行社的电邮回覆将作为接受英航团体票价合同的凭证，并同意合同内的条款。

AGENCY will confirm the acceptance of the contract to BA by email. The email reply will be the proof of AGENCY accepting the terms in the BA Group Fare Contract.

4. 
旅行社签定
80%合同后,
已确认的团队位子在任何情况下也不能转换到其他合同形式。

The confirmed group seats are not allowed to switch to other types of contract after the 80% commitment contract being signed by AGENCY.

第
**2** 条净票价

Paragraph 2 Net Fares
=====================

1. 
旅行社将从英航收到团体票价以及机位确认。

AGENCY will receive group fares from BA together with seats confirmation.

2．团体票价不包含任何费用和税费，该费用和税费将由旅行社相应收取并转账给英航。

The group fare does not include any fees and taxes, which accordingly have to be levied by AGENCY and transferred to BA.

3．本合同适用于在合同期内
5个团队(第二季度)或以上的预订 (每团至少
10 人)
。如果最后成行少于5个团队,
那将以单团的票价及规则作最后结算。

The contract applies for 5 or more group bookings (minimum 10 passengers per group departure between 01APR20-30JUN20) in the above period. If agent fails to fulfil the minimum of 5 groups, the ad hoc group fares and policy will be applied.

第
**3** 条预订和确认

Paragraph 3 Reservations and Confirmation
=========================================

1.  
旅行社将通过电话、传真或电子邮件与英航团队组联系。

AGENCY will communicate with BA group desk by phone, fax or email.

2. 
英航团队组将通过电子邮件向旅行社确认机位。

BA group desk will send seats confirmation to AGENCY by email.

3. 
旅行社在团队出发前一个月提供团队名单,
以免团体预定被取消。

AGENCY needs to submit group name list one month before departure to avoid group seats being cancelled.

第
**4** 条更改及退票

Paragraph 4 Changes and Refund
==============================

1. 
更改团队出发日期并不允许,
因签证而造成的延误或损失,
英航并没有任何豁免安排.

Change of outbound travel date is not allowed. There is no exemption under any circumstances such as the delay of visa application.

2.
出发前,
更改航班或日期的收费为每人每次人民币
1200 元，外加服务费,
或需缴付更改机票时的税费差额。出发后,
旅游日期及行程并不允许更改。如客人取消行程或没有按预定的航班登机,
团队机票于出发后将视作无效。

Before departure, date change is permitted at CNY1, 200 per transaction, subject to service fee, taxes difference will be charged for ticket re-issue. After departure, change of travel date and routing is not permitted. Group ticket will be invalid after departure
if passenger cancel or no-show the flight as booked.

3.
团队机票不可退款,
票价以及燃油附加费不予以退回,
只有政府税及机场建设税将予以退回。因签证延误或客人身体状况等原因而造成的订位取消,
英航并没有任何豁免安排。出发前被拒签,
旅行社需于团队出发日起5天内提供完整的拒签信,
逾期恕不受理,
英航将审核有关证明并保留作出最终决定的权利

All group tickets are non-refundable which means the fare and fuel surcharge are not refundable only the taxes. There is no exemption under any circumstances such as visa delay or medical case. In case the cancellation was caused by the
refusal of visa application before departure, AGENCY needs to provide the visa refusal letter within 5 days after group departure. The final decision is British Airways sole discretion after viewing the proof provided and subject to refund fee.

第
**5** 条票务及银行结算方案

Paragraph 5 Ticketing and Bank Settlement Plan (BSP)
====================================================

1.  
英航和旅行社同意通过
BSP 进行相关团队的出票和结算。

BA and AGENCY agree that the tickets will be issued via BSP.

2. 
旅行社依照英航出票细则于团队出发前
10 天，以假定航段方式出票。

AGENCY needs to issue tickets 10 days prior departure by using passive segments and following BA ticket instructions.

第
**6** 条 **80%**的承诺条件

Paragraph 6 Conditions of 80% commitment
========================================

1.  
应旅行社要求，在
2020年
4 月
1 日至
2020 年
9 月
30 日期间，英航将保留至少5个团队的机位(第二季度)，这些将计算在
80%成行率的承诺数字中。

BA will block minimum 5 groups 
departure between 01APR20-30JUN20 for the period of 01APR2020 to 30SEP2020 upon AGENCY request and these will be included in the 80% commitment figure.

2. 
额外申请的团队机位将计算在
80%成行率的承诺数字中，英航将以电邮通知旅行社最新的团队机位总数。

Any additional group seats request will be included in the 80% commitment figure. BA will inform AGENCY of the updated figure by email.

3. 
旅行社同意在此期间将所有保留的机位的成行率达到
80%。

AGENCY has agreed to utilize a minimum of 80% of the total seats blocked within this period.

4. 
出发前被拒签而造成的订位取消将不会计算在80%成行率的承诺数字中,
英航保留作出最终决定的权利,
旅行社需于团队出发日起5天内提供完整的拒签信，逾期恕不受理,
英航将审核有关证明并作出最终决定。

In case the cancellation was caused by the refusal of visa application before departure, it will not include in the 80% commitment figure subject to the final decision of BA. AGENCY needs to provide the visa refusal letter within 5 days
after group departure. The final decision is British Airways sole discretion after viewing the proof provided.

5. 
如团队中有部分客人需要升级至更高的舱位,
旅行社需提前联系英航申请升舱位子,
旅行社另外预定或购买任何散客机票，将不会计算到团队的实际成行率。

If partial group passenger request upgrade to higher cabin, AGENCY should contact British Airways in advance for such request. FIT tickets will not be counted into group utilisation.

6．如果未能达到以上约定的
80%成行率，旅行社同意通过
ADM 形式赔偿英航，罚金按如下方法计算:

Failing to utilize the agreed 80%, AGENCY has agreed to reimburse BA, through the issuance of an ADM, with the penalties based on the levels below:

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 80%或以上 |  | 没有罚金 |
| 70%-79% |  | 每个机位需赔付平均票价的 10% |
| 60%-69% |  | 每个机位需赔付平均票价的 25% |
| 59%或以下 |  | 每个机位需赔付平均票价的 50% |

（此种计算方式适用于常规票价机位）

7. 团队机位的优化管理：

任意一个英航系列团机位都被要求至少提前30天释放未售出的机位，即30天内完成100%出票率，未出票部分将会被收取CNY300/机位的额外罚金。

 

Each BA series groups is required to release the unsold seats at least 30days before the outbound date(100% utilization within 30days), CNY300/per seat will be charged as the extra penalty.

 

第
**7** 条保密

Paragraph 7 Confidentiality
===========================

1．双方同意对本合同内容及团队票价严格保密。

Both parties agree to keep the contents of this contract and the group fares strictly confidential.

第
**8** 条合同修改

Paragraph 8 Amendment to the Contract
=====================================

1．对本合同或其附件所做的任何修改或补充必须以电邮确认方能生效。本合同或其附件没有任何附属协议。

Any modification or addition to this agreement, or the annexes, must be confirmed through email in order to become effective. Any subsidiary agreement hereto or to annexes does not exist.

本合同的条款及细则之中,
如有任何争义,
英航保留最终决定权。

All matters and disputes will be subject to the final decision of British Airways.
