如何记住英语中各种小动物的叫声？

英语学习

我之前写过一篇《[还记得A puma at large吗？（文末有彩蛋）](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzAxNDc3ODQxNg==&mid=2247483718&idx=1&sn=6bc0f8ca3702769ee8471eafdd0036c1&chksm=9b8f6819acf8e10f0cee612ec2a62f3256bab9c47e5fec92754f58732d0e6cf7efece8837df7&scene=21#wechat_redirect)》，提及了英语中动物妈妈和动物宝宝的表达区分。当时就有校友们给我提出要求，让我还可以写写英语中各种动物的叫声汇总。

在英语启蒙阶段（[向英语机构偷师](http://mp.weixin.qq.com/s?__biz=MzAxNDc3ODQxNg==&mid=2247483807&idx=1&sn=211c31f61cb948f77f0e82960a49cf69&chksm=9b8f68c0acf8e1d6e3fbc5cffc753ae6e3e866b54dc9ca87ae62e8d59a1305a512f0c06a8939&scene=21#wechat_redirect)），我家老大特别喜欢一首动物叫的儿歌，跟着我又唱又跳。

歌词是这样的：

I'm a cat, meow, meow, meow.

我是猫，喵喵喵。

I'm a dog, woof, woof, woof.

我是狗，汪汪汪。

I'm a bear, I'm a mouse.

我是小熊，我是老鼠。

I'm a rabbit, jump jump jump.

我是小兔，跳跳跳。

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/5DBBC703-F85E-453D-AE7F-2C8B558C525D.jpg)

老大向我提出：中国汪星人的叫声是“汪汪”，而英国汪星人为什么却是像狼一样“呜夫呜夫”地叫呢。由此可见，中文里动物的叫声和英文并不一一对应。

如何才能便于孩子记住英语中小动物的叫声呢？我**最推荐的方法就是唱英语歌**或唱儿歌。

例如，提及蜜蜂的叫声就有这样一首儿歌。

Little bee, little bee, round, round, round.

小蜜蜂，小蜜蜂，转转转 。

Little bee, little bee, sound, sound, sound.

小蜜蜂 ，小蜜蜂，发声音 。

buzzzz~

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/A79C42D2-2AC8-408D-8367-A5F5BE668177.jpg)

所以，老大就在小蜜蜂边飞边舞中记住了小蜜蜂的声音是buzz。

还有一首耳熟能详的经典好歌**Old MacDonald Had A Farm**，基本囊括了大部分小动物的叫声。这首歌旋律欢快活泼，听一遍就可以让孩子记住副歌部分“E-I-E-I-O 咿呀咿呀呦”。歌曲分成多段，但大多语句反复出现，非常适合幼儿的年龄特点。

原曲很长，摘取主要循环部分如下：

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/5294AB14-21D3-4006-94B2-70B6AABFE7A2.jpg)

Old Macdonald had a farm, E-I-E-I-O.

And on that farm he had some cows, E-I-E-I-O.

With a "moo-moo" here and a "moo-moo" there.

Here a "moo", there a "moo"

Everywhere a "moo-moo".

Old Macdonald had a farm, E-I-E-I-O.

奶牛Cow的叫声为“moo-moo”，之后的歌词分别代入：鸭子Duck的叫声为“quack-quack”，小鸡Chick的叫声为“chick-chick”，小猪Pig的叫声为“oink-oink”，小羊Sheep的叫声为“baa-baa”，小马Horse的叫声为“neigh-neigh”。

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/4A880207-81AA-4350-8F2A-AF30821A5620.jpg)

其中，**各种“鸡”的叫声还各不相同，公鸡Cock, Rooster的叫声为“cock-a-doodle-doo”，母鸡Hen的叫声为“cluck-cluck”，小鸡Chick的叫声就是歌曲中提到的“chick-chick”。**

最后，再来介绍一首汇总了动物叫声的歌曲，歌曲名就叫“**The Animal Sounds Song** ”。个人觉得，只要把这首歌学会，大路货动物的叫声应该就掌握齐全了。

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/2BA4B2EB-C4F3-4370-8466-34303A6ACAFF.jpg)

The **dog** goes **woof** woof woof woof,

The **cow** goes **moo** moo moo moo,

The **duck** goes **quack** quack quack quack,

And the **owl** says to whit to **whoo**oo,

The **cat** goes **meow** meow meow meow,

The **bird** goes **tweet** tweet tweet tweet,

The **pig** goes **oink** oink oink oink,

And the little **mouse** says **squeak** squeak squeak,

(Squeak squeak squeak)

These are the sounds that animals make,

These are the sounds that animals make,

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/E2EB6A68-456D-4218-BB5A-5DE40F98C16C.jpg)

The **horse** goes **neigh** neigh neigh,

The **sheep** goes **baa** baa baa baa,

The **rabbit** goes **thump** thump thump,

（兔子一般叫声也是“**squeak**-squeak”）

And the people they say bla bla bla bla bla,

The **snake** goes **hiss** hiss hiss,

The **chicken** goes **cluck** cluck cluck cluck,

The **frog** goes **ribbit** ribbit ribbit,

The **bee**s say **buzz** buzz buzz buzz buzz buzz,

These are the sounds that animals make,

These are the sounds that animals make,

![](.evernote-content/D6FEEF42-77CB-4369-B809-1070866DB66C/7D542472-B6BE-4067-8012-46C79F527518.jpg)

The **donkey** goes **haw hee** haw hee haw,

The **elephent** goes **pawoooo**,

The **bear** growls**grrr** grrr,

The **lion** roars **raaaar**,

These are the sounds that animals make,

These are the sounds that animals make,

These are the sounds that animals make,

These are the sounds that animals make.
