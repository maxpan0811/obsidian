**TMC是什么？**

![](.evernote-content/9D51CB0C-4C54-4C1E-A699-180CE9B4A40C/8295D241-010E-4B62-9806-D1DF0B68C03A.png)

TMC ( Think Math Challenge )是由ACME ( American Center of Mathematics Education ) 举办的一项国际数学赛事，旨在激发学生的求知欲望，提高学习兴趣，促进思维能力的发展，培养良好的思维品质、探索精神和创造才能；发现、选拔、培养智力超常，有数学天赋，对数学热爱的青少年，为广大数学爱好者提供交流和展示自我的舞台。

TMC (Think Math Challenge) is an international mathematics competition organized by ACME (American Center of Mathematics Education). The competition aims to inspire students' curiosity and enhance their interest in learning, promote the development of their thinking abilities, cultivate good thinking qualities, exploratory spirit, and creative talent. It also aims to identify, select, and cultivate intellectually gifted and mathematically talented young people who have a passion for mathematics, providing a platform for communication and self-expression for math enthusiasts around the world.

参赛选手来自多个国家和地区：美国、英国、德国、加拿大、澳大利亚、新西兰、新加坡、马来西亚、中国香港等，**试题提供中英双语版本**，适合全球范围内使用英文或中文的数学学习者。各等级Top 10选手也将有机会获邀参加明年暑假的全球总决赛。

The contestants come from multiple countries and regions, including the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Malaysia, the Philippines, Vietnam, and Hong Kong China. **The test provides bilingual versions in Chinese and English**, making it suitable for mathematics learners around the world who use either English or Chinese. Top 10 players of each level will stand a chance to receive invitations to participate in the final globals next summer.

**Grade 4**，在读4年级且年龄不超过10.5周岁

**Grade 5**，在读5年级且年龄不超过11.5周岁

**Grade 6**，在读6年级且年龄不超过12.5周岁

**Grade 7**，在读7年级且年龄不超过13.5周岁

如有所在国家年级和学制不同，请参照以上要求报名合适等级。赛事允许向上跨级，但请勿报名更低等级。

The competition comprises 7 levels for students of different ages:

**Grade 1**, (for age 7.5 and below)

**Grade 2**, (for age 8.5 and below)

**Grade 3**, (for age 9.5 and below)

**Grade 4**, (for age 10.5 and below)

**Grade 5**, (for age 11.5 and below)

**Grade 6**, (for age 12.5 and below)

**Grade 7**, (for age 13.5 and below)

Please adhere to these criteria for registration, irrespective of your country's educational grading system. Competing at higher levels is permitted, but please refrain from registering for lower ones.

**2**

**评分标准**

**试题为中英双语；1、2年级提供双语读题**

**比赛时长：**

75mins

**比赛题型：**

1～5题为选择题，

6～15题为填空题，

答案为0～999999之间的整数。

**评分规则：**

共15题，

1～5题每题5分，

6～10题每题6分，

11～13题每题7分，

14～15题每题12分。

满分100分，

答对得分，答错不扣分。

The test questions are provided in bilingual versions in both Chinese and English, and for grades 1 and 2, bilingual audio reading assistance is also provided.

**Duration of the Contest**：

75 mins

**Test Format** : 

multiple-choice questions 1-5，

fill-in-the-blank questions 6-15 ,

answers shall be integers between 0 and 999999.

**Grading Policy**:

Question 1-5: 5 points per question;

Question 6-10: 6 points per question;

Question 11-13: 7 points per question.

Question 14-15: 12 points per question. 

Full mark is 100,

wrong answers would not deduct grade points.
