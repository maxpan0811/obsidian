#### 生平

[![](.evernote-content/84E3E9B6-C8D2-4006-8853-E982978512A1/E4C9CE99-67F1-499A-A550-062C90D1BEAD.jpg) 书法家启功先生 图册 23张](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/image/96dda144ad3459824c3ea0230cf431adcaef84cf?lemmaid=16331&sublemmaid=15740193&from=album&bk_fr=bk_view&uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&bk_fr=bk_view&imgmod=0) 1933年经[傅增湘](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/487354.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)先生推介，受业于[陈垣](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/2022.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)，涉足[学术](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/483967.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)流别与[考证](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/683013.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)之学。后聘为[辅仁中学](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/1232895.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)国文[教员](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/777970.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)。

1935年任[辅仁大学](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/513009.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)美术系[助教](http://wapbaike.baidu.com/view/370722.htm?uid=63A05718085FC3C83A058658BA8907FB&ssid=0&pu=usm@4,sz@1320_2001,ta@iphone_1_9.1_3_601&bd_page_type=1&from=1099b&st=3&step=2&net=1&ldr=2&statwiki=1)。
