Astrill Setup Manual:How to quickly configure PPTP/ L2TP /IPSec with profile configuration install method on iPhone and iPod Touch
==================================================================================================================================

1. **Open Safari on your iPhone**, then login to **[Members](https://members.astrill.com/)** section. After that go to **[VPN Services](https://members.astrill.com/vpn-services.php)** and then **click on** **"iPhone/iPhone/iPod Configurations"**.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/FB5C556E-FFAF-474B-BE20-218C890BF6B1.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-001.jpg)

---

2. **Select what VPN locations and what VPN protocols** you would like to use on iPhone by switching **ON/OFF** button.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/C5943521-D03B-4B9F-977C-663757A65589.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-002.jpg)

---

3. After you have made the selection, scroll down and **click "Create the config file".**

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/FCB14294-F7C1-4F55-8295-E70DEE4A5886.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-003.jpg)

---

4. Mobileconfig file will be created, click on that link to **download to your iPhone.**

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/EAC9E0A8-50EC-4F77-879B-0D38FE512ED3.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-004.jpg)

---

5. You will see **"Install Profile"** screen, **tap on "Install" button**.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/9C6954A0-37D3-4BD1-BAEB-786ECE289EDE.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-005.jpg)

---

6. You will get a confirmation message box, tap on **"Install Now"** button.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/DB938191-14AC-4804-853C-EE8E8C6CFEF7.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-006.jpg)

---

7. You will see "Profile Installed" on the screen, **tap on "Done" button.**

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/AEF68843-18AA-4E81-AEDE-F38FEB913C27.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-007.jpg)

---

8. Go to main screen, then **tap on "Settings" icon.**

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/3616573D-0BCC-4F40-8CA1-88F9D3175302.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-008.jpg)

---

9. On the Settings screen **tap on "VPN" item.**

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/B503B60F-5ED7-4799-822D-9B40E664C7A7.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-009.jpg)

---

10. You should see all VPN locations and protocols that you have installed on "Astrill VPN" profile. **Select the VPN** you would like to connect to, and **then tap on "OFF"** button to start the VPN connection.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/0D6D2782-2EAC-4C36-9A74-184516267D04.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-010.jpg)

---

11. Once connected, you should see **status "Connected"** and VPN icon on top bar.

[![](.evernote-content/3367C650-DFE6-40E8-86C4-DE43B651F090/FC2A5FBD-7B63-4772-8653-1634D72CB489.jpg)](http://wiki.astrill.com/index.php/File:Astrill-vpn-profile-on-iphone-011.jpg)

---

**NOTE: When using PPTP/L2TP/IPSec VPN over 3G connection, make sure proxy settings are disabled. As it's likely these protocols can't work over proxy.**
