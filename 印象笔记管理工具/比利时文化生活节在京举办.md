比利时文化生活节在京举办
============

比利时驻华大使馆 比利时驻华大使馆

![](.evernote-content/BE507F32-B6DC-45BC-B606-7A65F515BFB8/757EC6FD-07E2-485A-B145-79382EFEE0DF.png)

7月22日-24日，为期三天的“会聚比利时—比利时文化生活节”在缦合·北京华府会隆重举行。正值7月21日比利时国庆日，由音合文化与缦合·北京华府会联合举办的文化生活节，通过多元化的形式，以涉及生活各个领域、品类众多的比利时经典品牌产品为窗口，全方位展现比利时文化生活的方方面面，为北京提供了一个周末出行好去处，也让我们足不出户便能体验到纯正的比利时文化，感受传统节庆日的欢乐氛围。

From 22 to 24 July, the “Belgian Culture and Lifestyle Festival” was held inside Maha Beijing at the Hopson Club. Co-organized by Interlace and the Hopson Club right after Belgian National Day was celebrated on 21 July, the event showcased all aspects of Belgian culture and lifestyle by presenting a diverse range of Belgian brands. The event provided a fun weekend getaway in Beijing. It was a great way to experience Belgium in all its authenticity, as well as the joys of celebrating the Belgian National Day, without having to travel far from home.

![](.evernote-content/BE507F32-B6DC-45BC-B606-7A65F515BFB8/820713EE-DEE3-4066-B666-8069991341A2.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/BE507F32-B6DC-45BC-B606-7A65F515BFB8/69187FFF-81D2-45AC-800C-7D0F51D6F342.jpg)

7月22日晚间的开幕式上，比利时驻华大使高洋博士（H.E.Dr. Jan Hoogmartens）强调，在7月21日比利时国庆日这个特殊的时间节点，通过这种文化生活交流的方式，将比利时的文化及优质产品展现在中国朋友面前意义重大，并预祝本次文化生活节圆满举行。

At the opening ceremony on 22 July, the Belgian Ambassador in China, Dr. Jan Hoogmartens, pronounced a speech about the significance of introducing Chinese friends to the culture and the high-quality products of Belgium through such an event and during the National Day festivities, then further wished the festival a successful outcome.

![](.evernote-content/BE507F32-B6DC-45BC-B606-7A65F515BFB8/9BA552FD-C020-4FE8-A0B4-BECA97E2EEB6.jpg)

![](.evernote-content/BE507F32-B6DC-45BC-B606-7A65F515BFB8/4F67147F-EE6E-4BA4-B8C4-E55D61E790AC.jpg)

本次文化生活节活动现场分为文化市集、文化活动和主题展览三个部分。文化市集为期三日，十数家参展商和囊括了食品饮料、时尚产品、个人护理和文化出版物等近50个比利时知名品牌，对于中国消费者来说是体验比利时历史悠久的工匠精神、丰富多样的生活文化产品的最佳时机。无论是爱好啤酒、巧克力、冰淇淋的资深饕客、还是想要了解更多比利时生活细节的旅游达人，都能在这里感受到来源于西欧的纯正异国风情。

The festival was divided into three parts: a cultural market, a series of cultural events and thematic exhibitions. The three-day event, with over a dozen exhibitors and near 50 famous Belgian brands in food and beverage, fashion, personal care and cultural publications was the perfect opportunity for Chinese consumers to experience the Belgium’s centuries-old artisanship and its rich variety in lifestyle and cultural products. Whether you already are a seasoned beer, chocolate or ice cream lover, or a traveller looking for more information about Belgium, you will be able to experience the new and foreign flavours from Western Europe.

本次活动分会场安排了内容多样的文化活动，从旅行分享会、读书会；到巧克力、啤酒的品鉴会，不一而足。

Various cultural activities were organised at the event’s venues, ranging from travelling stories and book readings to chocolate and beer tasting sessions.
