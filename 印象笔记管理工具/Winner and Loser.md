# Winner and Loser

---

Winner vs Loser

成功者与失败者

The Winner is always part of the answer;

成功者是解答的一部份；

The Loser is always part of the problem.

失败者是问题的一部分。

The Winner is always has a program;

成功者总有计划；

The Loser always has an excuse.

失败者总有理由。

The Winner says,"Let me do it for you;

嬴家说：“让我来做”；

The Loser says;" That is not my job."

失败者说：“那不是我的工作”。

The Winner sees an answer for every problem;

成功者总是从难题中看到答案；

The Loser sees a problem for every answer.

失败者总是从答案中看到困难。

The Winner says," It may be difficult but it is possible";

成功者说：“或许困难，但可以做”；

The Loser says,"It may be possible but it is too difficult."

失败者说：“或许可以做，但太困难”。

When a Winner makes a mistake, he says," I was wrong";

成功者犯错误时，他说：“我错了”；

When a Loser makes a mistake, he says," It wasn't my fault."

失败者犯错误时，他说：“不是我的错”

A Winner makes commitments;

成功者做保证；

A Loser makes promises.

失败者常许诺。

Winners have dreams;

成功者有梦想；

Loser have schemes.

失败者有日程。

Winners say," I must do something";

成功者说：“我必须做某事”；

Losers say,"Something must be done."

失败者说：“某件事必须做”。

Winners are a part of the team;

成功者是团队的一部分；

Losers are apart from the team.

而失败者总是远离团队。

Winners see the gain;

成功者看到收获；

Losers see the pain.

失败者看到痛苦。

Winners see possibilities;

成功者看到可能性；

Losers see problems.

失败者看到困难。

Winners believe in win/win;

成功者相信双赢；

Losers believe for them to win someone has to lose.

失败者认为非赢必输。

Winners see the potential;

成功者看到未来；

Losers see the past.

失败者看到过去。

Winners are like a thermostat;

成功者像恒温器；

Losers are like thermometers.

失败者像温度计。

Winners choose what they say;

成功者怎么说怎么做；

Losers say what they choose.

失败者怎么做怎么说。

Winners use hard arguments but soft words;

成功者观点鲜明但言语温和；

Losers use soft arguments but hard words.

失败者观点不充分但言语生硬。

Winners stand firm on s but compromise on petty things;

成功者坚持价值观，不在小事情上纠缠；

Losers stand firm on petty things but compromise on s.

失败者在小事情上纠缠，但在价值观上妥协。

Winners follow the philosophy of empathy: "Don't do to others what you

would, not want them to do to you";

成功者的情感哲学是“已所不欲，勿加于人”

Losers follow the philosophy, "Do it to others before they do it to you."

失败者的情感哲学是:“先下手为强”

Winners make it happen;

成功者制造可能，

Losers let it happen.

失败者坐以待毙。
