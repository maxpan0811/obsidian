中高GI值水果，则包括龙眼、[芒果](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E8%8A%92%E6%9E%9C&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、[木瓜](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E6%9C%A8%E7%93%9C&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、[西瓜](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E8%A5%BF%E7%93%9C&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、[文旦](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E6%96%87%E6%97%A6&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、香蕉、[凤梨](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E5%87%A4%E6%A2%A8&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、[葡萄](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E8%91%A1%E8%90%84&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)、[荔枝](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E8%8D%94%E6%9E%9D&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)等。

GI值指的是血糖值上升率，也就是当我们将食物吃进体内后，(相对于吃进葡萄糖时)使血糖升高的比例。简单来说，越容易使血糖快速上升的食物，其GI值就越高，像醣类和淀粉类是属于高GI；反之，使血糖上升速度较慢的食物，其GI值就越低，像油[脂类](https://m.baidu.com/s?word=%E8%84%82%E7%B1%BB&from=1018914a&lqsource=-1&dmaseid=dmaseid95009&qid=00000000c9efdf87&sa=gh_wise_zhidao)和蛋白质属于低GI。血糖偏高的人群比较适宜低GI饮食。(最高为葡萄糖，GI值=100)

低GI值水果，有苹果、芭乐、番茄、桃子、葡萄柚、水梨、奇异果等；
