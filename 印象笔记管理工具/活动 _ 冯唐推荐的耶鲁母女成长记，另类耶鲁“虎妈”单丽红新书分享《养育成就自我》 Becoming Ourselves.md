**活动介绍**

The Event

在人工智能时代，教育的意义正在被重新审视，未来的教育该培养怎样的精英人才？11月6日，**耶鲁管理学院’97届校友、耶鲁北京校友会顾问、铂桥咨询联合创始人和董事长单丽红**携新书《养育成就自我——耶鲁母女成长记》做客耶鲁北京中心，分享她从培养两个女儿先后迈进**耶鲁本科学院**的成长过程中思考出的教育真谛和女性自我完善心得，并将与北京四中国际校区副校长王实展开深度对谈。对谈后还设有现场签售环节。

![](.evernote-content/B944EC12-79AB-41B7-8C56-5CC63E7E3B09/1CD8FBB7-55EA-461C-8677-5AEB50CFEFC2.png)

《养育成就自我——耶鲁母女成长记》

作者：单丽红

2025年10月由上海文艺出版社出版

In the age of AI, the significance of education is being reexamined, how will future education shape elite talents?**Sally Shan MBA ’97, Advisor of the Yale Club of Beijing and Co-Founder & Chairperson of AspenBridge Advisory**, explores this question through her personal journey as a mother of two daughters—both admitted to **Yale College**—in her new book, ***Becoming Ourselves—A Yale Mother-Daughter Story of Growth***. 

On November 6, Sally Shan will join Shi Wang, Vice Principal of the International Division of Beijing No. 4 High School, at Yale Center Beijing for an in-depth conversation inspired by the ways she and her two daughters have grown together, offering insights on women’s self-improvement. A book signing with Sally Shan will follow the discussion.

“老友Sally在养闺女的过程中又把自己养了一遍。一段时光，母女三人，一枝三朵，花开灿烂。想做好父母的，有空翻翻这另类虎妈的浅吟低唱。”

——作家冯唐

Speakers

**单丽红**

**耶鲁管理学院’97届校友**

**耶鲁北京校友会顾问**

**铂桥咨询联合创始人、董事长**

单丽红现任铂桥咨询联合创始人和董事长。2012至2024年，她曾担任HarbourVest中国区总裁，建立了私募股权投资和募资平台。此前，她还担任过摩根大通董事总经理及亚洲科技投行部主管，并在硅谷和纽约的雷曼兄弟全球科技投行部任职十年。她于1992年获得中国人民大学经济学学士学位，于1997年获得耶鲁大学MBA学位。最近，她出版了新书《养育成就自我——耶鲁母女成长记》。

**Sally Shan MBA ’97**

**Advisor, Yale Club of Beijing**

**Co-Founder & Chairman, AspenBridge Advisory**

Sally Shan is the Co-Founder and Chairman of AspenBridge Advisory. She was Head of China at HarbourVest Partners from 2012 to 2024, where she built the private equity investment and fundraising platform. Previously, she served in the Global Technology Investment Banking Group of Lehman Brothers in Silicon Valley and New York for ten years and she also served as Managing Director and Head of Asia Technology Investment Banking at JPMorgan. She received a BS in economics from the Renmin University of China in 1992 and an MBA from Yale University in 1997. She is the author of Becoming Ourselves—A Yale Mother-Daughter Story of Growth.

**王实 Shi Wang**

![](.evernote-content/B944EC12-79AB-41B7-8C56-5CC63E7E3B09/A1E33816-C5CA-441B-AF6F-589433B0898E.png)

**王实**

**北京四中国际校区副校长**

****美国教育考试服务中心托福考试理事会成员****

王实，资深国际教育专家，现任北京四中国际校区副校长。他曾任美国大学理事会College Board首届全球国际顾问委员会顾问，也是EducationUSA杰出贡献奖获得者和特别顾问、升学指导研究中心联合创始人（ChinaICAC）兼执行委员会委员。他是畅销书《实话实说帮你实现美国留学》的作者，并多次在《纽约时报》中文版发表中国国际教育专题文章。

**Shi Wang**

**Vice Principal, Beijing No.4 High School International Campus**

****Board Member, Educational Testing Service (ETS)****

Shi Wang, a well-known international education expert who is serving as Vice Principal at the Beijing No.4 High School International Campus. He was a former member of the International Advisory Committee of the College Board, winner of “Outstanding Contribution Award” and special advisor of Education USA, and co-founder of ChinaICAC. He has published articles on China's international education in major media outlets including the*New York Times* and is the author of a best seller on college counseling in Chinese.
