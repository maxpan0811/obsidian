Rowing give children physical challenges and difficulties.Rowing is similar with other achievements, you need to pay huge amounts of energy, even endure a long time of patience; We never tell the children how to view the world, we inspired them to find the key to the world through classroom guidance and equal communication.

**环保健康**

**(Environmental protection**

**and health)**

赛艇运动属于速度耐力项目。经常参加赛艇运动，能有效地增强人体心血管和呼吸系统功能，发展全身肌肉的力量和耐力。赛艇运动多在江河湖泊等大自然水域中进行，空气清新，阳光充足，受到大自然的沐浴，能有效地提高人体的心血管和呼吸系统功能。除此以外，赛艇运动员的肺活量在各项体育项目中也是占到了第一位，更有人把赛艇运动称为肺部体操。

Rowing belongs Speed Endurance Games. Always participating in rowing can enhance the human cardiovascular and respiratory system effectively,develop muscle strength and endurance.Rowing will be carried out in the rivers, lakes and other natural waters.Fresh air, plenty of sunshine, nature bath, can improve the cardiovascular and respiratory functions of the human body.In addition, the rowers'lung capacity is the first one of the sports events. It was called the exercises of lungs. 

![](.evernote-content/DBAFD0BC-AB8A-4F75-BD2F-53955D4EAE43/9C670ED6-4699-458D-BF77-CD6630B00A2B.jpg)

**团队精神**

**（Teamwork）**

赛艇运动不崇尚个人英雄主义，强调集体协作，队员之间如何协调，这是这项运动的精髓。在赛艇运动中，不管是坐在哪个位置、负责哪项任务，只有团结一心，才能最终驾驭赛艇一路前行，培养人们坚韧不拔的意志和集体主义精神。

Rowing doesn't advocate individual heroism, it emphasize teamwork. How to coordinate with teammate, this is the essence of this sport. On the way of rowing, whether which position you sit, whether which tasks you take,teamwork is the only way to control the boat all the way. It cultivate children' s indomitable will and spirit of collectivism.

**国际化**

**（Internationalization）**

牛津剑桥赛艇比赛每年都能吸引25万人到泰晤士河观战，600多万英国人会通过电视或者电台转播关注比赛，从1829年开始至今从未间断。三任俄罗斯总统的普金先生、被国际誉为“现代奥林匹克之父”的顾拜旦先生都是出色的赛艇运动员。赛艇所象征的奋斗与合作精神是当今国际化、多元化的社会交流中越来越必要的素质。

The rowing competition of Oxford and Cambridge attracted 250,000 spectators to the River Thames every year,over 600 million Britons concerned about the competition on television or radio.From the beginning of 1829, it has never stopped.Russian President Vladimir Putin, Coubertin,who is known as "the father of the modern Olympic",they are all excellent rowers.The struggle and the spirit of cooperation becomes increasingly necessary qualities of the current international, diversified social communication.

赛艇运动是这样一种运动，它要求互相合作、积极配合、相互包容、面对未来。

Rowing is such a motion, which requires mutual cooperation, active cooperation, mutual tolerance, facing the future.

——王石

Wang Shi
